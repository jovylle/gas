Charts & Iran war baseline

GlobalPetrolPrices publishes percent changes since Feb 23, 2026 (their last weekly point before the Iran war). We scrape that table to compute implied pre-war prices in the same currency as today: price ÷ (1 + %/100). Current prices come from OpenVan.
